Breakfast Sandwich Recipe

Description

This breakfast sandwich is a perfect start to your day, combining eggs, cheese, and your choice of meat on a toasted bun.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg cooked any way (Use raw at your own risk)
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 1 slice of ham or bacon
  • 1 toasted bun or bagel
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the egg in a skillet over medium heat until the yolk is set.
  2. Add the cheese on top of the egg and let it melt.
  3. Cook the ham or bacon until crispy.
  4. Assemble the sandwich by placing the egg and cheese on the toasted bun, followed by the meat.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy your delicious breakfast sandwich!
  7. Optional: Add avocado, tomato, or hot sauce for extra flavor.
