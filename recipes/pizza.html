Pizza Recipe

Description

This is a classic pizza recipe that you can make at home. It's simple and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, salt, and sugar.
  2. Dissolve yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  3. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the flour mixture. Knead until smooth.
  4. Cover the dough and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour.
  5. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  6. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  7. Spread pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  8. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  9. Add fresh basil leaves, salt, and pepper to taste.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
  11. Remove from oven, slice, and enjoy your homemade pizza!
