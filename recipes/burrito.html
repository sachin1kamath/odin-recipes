Burrito Recipe

Description

This is a delicious burrito recipe that you can easily make at home. It's packed with flavor and perfect for a quick meal! (See above for more detail)

Ingredients

  • 1 large flour tortilla
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken or beef, shredded
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup guacamole
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Any other extra fillings (Roasted tomatoes, onions, sauces, etc.)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Warm the tortilla in a skillet over medium heat for about 30 seconds on each side.
  2. In the center of the tortilla, layer the cooked rice, black beans, shredded chicken or beef, and cheese.
  3. Drizzle salsa over the top and add sour cream and guacamole.
  4. Sprinkle with chopped cilantro, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling, then roll it up tightly from the bottom to the top.
  6. Place the burrito seam-side down in the skillet and cook for about 2-3 minutes until golden brown and heated through.
  7. Remove from the skillet, slice in half, and serve with extra salsa, sour cream, or guacamole on the side.
  8. Enjoy your homemade burrito!
